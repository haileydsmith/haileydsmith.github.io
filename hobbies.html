Hobbies

Volleyball Fitness Knitting Crafts Diet

Having hobbies and a variety of hobbies is really important to me. My brain is constantly juggling different and creating new ideas all the time. I do work a large portion of the day and week so having something to do outside of work has become a healthy necessity. It is easy for me to become not only overwhelmed but knee deep in work. I have laid awake at night thinking about all the work I have to the next day or week. There have been times I even dream about work and all the things I need to do the next day. It became very real that I needed to have things in my life separate from working. Having an outlet outside of work to distract myself has really improved my quality of life and mental health. It is important for me to be healthy so a lot of my hobbies are fitness and diet orientated. I love to challenge myself with new exercises or simply do something because its fun. I also really love to create things and I constantly have the itch to be imaginative. Having multiple outlets for myself to be creative is important so that I never get bored.
