My grandma taught me to knit at a really young age but I never really got into it. When I was away at college, I had the idea of knitting Christmas gifts. Taking my brief experience with my grandma and a lot of YouTube videos I taught myself how to knit. I started to make a lot of baby gifts and ended up starting to sell things online. Now that I work full time I took a step back from my business and focused on knitting gifts and fun things for my friends and self.
